* Stealing has no speed threshold.
* Merlinus is not needed to send items to the convoy. However, the convoy cannot be accessed without Merlinus on the map.
* All Legendary weapons are needed to obtain the "true" ending.
* You can't access the battle preparations shop in Hard Mode.
* How do Hard Mode Bonuses work? Well, the folks over at triangleattack explain it better than I can, so head over to https://fe6.triangleattack.com/guides/hard-mode-bonuses
